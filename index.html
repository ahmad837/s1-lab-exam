Cryptosomniac is a platform perfect for all those who want to be part of the digital currency era. Cryptosomniac offers both free and paid crypto services. The free services include cryptocurrency price tracker, crypto portfolio management tool, detailed analytical reports & guides and latest news curated from all over the globe. The paid services include exclusive content, one-on-one consultancy appointments, and video courses.

Porter's Barber

Porter's Barber is an online portal for top-class men’s grooming establishment. This website is packed with powerful features and functionalities. It is fully integrated with social media platforms, has a secure web-based store with multiple payment methods and comes with a consistent UI that makes online booking a simple and easy task. This site allows users to buy 350 club membership and Porter's merchandize in less than a minute.

StoryStar

StoryStar is a free online website where short story aficionados can write and publish their work. Visitors can read the work of other short story authors free of cost. StoryStar has all aspects of digital marketing covered from on-page SEO to newsletter integration. Loyal users can make contributions via secure payment methods. The site has an extensive admin panel that allows administrators to moderate site activity.
